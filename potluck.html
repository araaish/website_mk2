Potluck Recommendations

Potluck Recommendations is a Google Chrome extension that improves the Youtube Recommendations experience. The extension allows you to set a limit on the number of recommended videos displayed on the homepage. Aside from Youtube algorithm based recommendations, Potluck Recommendations also displays videos that your friends from your Google contacts have recommended. You can also recommend one video to your friends.

Get Potluck Recommendations from the Chrome web store

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Potluck Recommendations accesses, uses, stores, or shares Google user data.

1. Data Usage Explanation

The extension collects and processes user data to provide a better app experience. This includes the following:

  • User's Google account information
  • User's Google contacts
  • User's Youtube video recommendations

    • This extension does not collect or process any other user data. The user's Google account email address and video url recommendation are stored in the extension's database. Only the user's video recommendation can be viewed by their Google contacts.

    The user's Google account address is used to find their Google contacts. The user's Google contacts are used to find associated recommendations. The user's Youtube video recommendations are stored to share with their Google contacts. The user's Google account address is also used to identify their recommended video.

    This extension does not share user data with any third parties.

    2. Enhancing User Functionality

    Potluck Recommendations enhances user functionality by providing a better Youtube Recommendations experience. The recommendations collected by Potluck are used to curate a more thoughtful and personalized selection of videos. The user can more effectively limit fatigue from excessive consumption of Youtube content, while improving the quality of content through their social network.
