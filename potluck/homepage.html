Potluck Recommendations Homepage

Potluck Recommendations is a Google Chrome extension that improves the Youtube Recommendations experience.

Get Potluck Recommendations from the Chrome web store

Functionality

The extension allows you to set a limit on the number of recommended videos displayed on the homepage. Aside from Youtube algorithm based recommendations, Potluck Recommendations also displays videos that your friends from your Google contacts have recommended. You can also recommend one video to your friends.
